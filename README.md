# ROSTEPLOCENTR

Official website of **ROSTEPLOCENTR**.

ROSTEPLOCENTR provides engineering equipment supply and installation of internal engineering systems for residential and commercial projects.

### Main areas

- Gas boilers
- Heating radiators
- PPR pipes and fittings
- Sewer systems
- Technical and construction doors
- Metal components for flue and chimney systems

The company works with private clients, contractors and construction projects.
